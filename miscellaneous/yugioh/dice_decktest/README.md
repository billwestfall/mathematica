rules for dicedecktest:

1)  have a large pool of Yugioh cards with good split of levels and fair amount of traps and spells
2)  when you roll for the AI, place token on field or take action as indidcated. AI should play first to balance the game
3)  for player, separate your cards into piles of a) spells b) traps c) monsters level 1-2, d) monsters level 3-4 e) monsters level 5-6 f) monsters 7+ g) extra deck monsters. shuffle each pile (don't shuffle them together though)
4)  roll for player and take card from designated pile. rolls are based on normal curve of average dice rolls of two six sided dice
